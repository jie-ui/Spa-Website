Benefits

Restores the skin’s natural radiance.

Cleanse | Exfoliation | Massage | Personalized

Allow yourself to let go of all that weighs on you. Breathe in. Breathe out. This treatment focuses on creating a profound sense of calm, inside and out, far away from daily worries. .

Your esthetician will carefully consider your skin type and personalize your care with practiced techniques and meticulously selected products.

This classic face care treatment includes a thorough cleanse, followed by exfoliation and extraction upon demand. Your esthetician will then provide a facial massage using cryotherapy spoons, as well as a neck and decolletage massage, followed by the application of a hydrating mask. This moment you have taken for yourself will end in pure bliss. A soothed mind and a glowing complexion are well within reach.

This treatment is safe during pregnancy and breastfeeding or can be adapted accordingly.
